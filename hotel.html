Zoi Boutique Hotel, Matemwe, ZanzibarThe perfect escape

Disconnect from the world and enjoy clean-lined Swahili architecture with a light, airy, and modern vibe against the picture-perfect natural backdrop. An inviting world of natural splendour can be found around one of Africa’s best sandy beaches. We’re also situated close to Zanzibar’s most fascinating sights and monuments and offer a plethora of flavoursome local cuisine crafted with locally sourced organic ingredients. Our eco hotel will allow you to discover a beachfront gem – paradise hidden on Earth.

Facilities & Services

Zoi Boutique Hotel is an inviting world where wellness is present in all shapes and forms. Your lifestyle experience and rejuvenation journey begins as you enter your oceanfront room or suite, where you will be welcomed by a palette of myriad shades of tranquil greens and blues.
