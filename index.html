Zoi Boutique Hotel, Matemwe, Zanzibar

The perfect escape

“Zoi” means life in Greek, and we honour the values of life balance, wellness, spiritual rebirth and connection with nature as our everyday ritual that we share with our guests. If you want to quench your thirst for life, Zoi Boutique Hotel offers you a regenerative hotel experience beyond expectations.
Nested on a long, sandy, white-sand beach in the unspoiled fishing village of Matemwe, Zoi Boutique Hotel offers an unparalleled experience of the stunning natural beauty of Zanzibar. Awash in biodiversity and trimmed with pristine beaches and greenery, our property is thoughtfully designed by ecological experts to interact with the natural environmental and cultural context of its surroundings. Each stay is a unique experience highlighted by unmatched natural beauty and tranquillity to put guests at ease.

Connect with nature

Surrounded by lush biodiversity that pays homage to its natural and cultural surroundings, Zoi Boutique Hotel invites you to explore nature in its purest form. Each of the sixteen spacious rooms and suites use stylish Swahili lines and shapes in an elegant presentation of Zanzibar’s calming powers. Large windows bathe the interiors with natural light while the soothing sound of the ocean eases the soul.

Theros Restaurant

Pure, fresh, and delicious

Matemwe Zanzibar’s organic cuisine emphasizes upon the culinary biodiversity of Zanzibar’s gastronomic past. Our oceanfront restaurant offers flavourful local cuisine with an international twist and mesmerizing ocean views over Matemwe’s unspoiled white-sand coast.
Refining the concept of Swahili fusion cuisine with a handpicked portfolio of locally sourced fresh fish, vegetables, fruits, and spices, the core of our restaurant’s menu lies in the heart and soul of Zanzibar.

Explore

dream destination

Zanzibar is a dream destination. Located in East Africa, Zanzibar is the biggest island close to Tanzania. It is famous for its long, white-sand beaches, corners and neighbourhoods where time appears to have slowed down, and its fast-paced Western civilization and lifestyle. An exotic land full of lush green forests, pristine turquoise waters, and unspoiled towns and fishing villages fill your soul with bliss.
