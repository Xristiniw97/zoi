Experiences Discover a world of wonders.

Prepare to be awash with serenity and gratitude when enjoying the unique travel experiences that Zanzibar has to offer.
Take your pick and be blessed with memorable moments that last for a lifetime.
A visit to the cheetah sanctuary is a must as well as a guided tour at the historic capital town of Zanzibar; Stone City, an UNESCO World Heritage Site the likes of which can’t be found anywhere else in the world.
The giant turtles at Prison Island are also an unmissable experience that guests enjoy during their stay.
Just allow our team of local experts and our dedicated day-trip planners handcraft your perfect daily itinerary so you can take in all the beauty and culture that Zanzibar has to offer.

MNEBA ISLAND

COLOURFUL REEFS AND UNDERWATER ADVENTURES

If you love colourful reefs and underwater adventures, Mneba island is a must. Voted as the #1 best beach destination in Africa, this paradise on Earth is just a short journey away from the property and offers an unforgettable experience to every guest. Enjoy stunning views of tropical reef fish, swim in the most crystal-clear waters in the Indian ocean, taste local exotic fruits, experience unique snorkelling tours to meet different species of fish such as angelfish, sea turtles, black snappers, groupers, frog fish, and, of course, dolphins. A day tour here will be one of the most amazing experiences you can have while staying at our hotel!
The tour to Mneba island is a full-day affair and includes lunch, private transportation, and guided excursions.

Prison Island - Changuu Private Island

EXPLORE THE UNIQUE SPECIES

Meet the giant Aldabra tortoises in their natural habitat in Prison Island, Zanzibar. Take a tour and explore the unique species on a stunning paradise on Earth surrounded by white beaches and breath-taking scenery. The old ruins of the prison have now been turned into bright living spaces with a cafe, boutique, and library.
The giant tortoises were brought here a century ago from Seychelles and are the only living creatures inhabiting the island today. The fascinating experience of meeting this ancient living animal on its own private island cannot be found anywhere else in the world.

Stone Town

UNESCO WORLD HERITAGE SITE

This UNESCO World Heritage Site is a must visit when in Zanzibar. A cultural travel experience that takes you back in time in the most authentic way. It is a fine example of Swahili coastal trading towns that remain untouched by time even today. African, Arab, Persian, Indian, and European elements create an incredible mosaic of culture, architecture, and history in an unparalleled, virtually intact townscape.
This is a fascinating and educational guided tour not only for those who are interested in history, culture, and architecture, but also for those who long to add unique travel experiences to their travel diary.

SCUBA DIVING

DIVEPOINT ZANZIBAR

Probably one of the best scuba diving and snorkelling destinations in Africa, Zanzibar’s scuba diving in is one of the most exciting experiences in the country. Its underwater world is a magical paradise. With various colourful fish and an abundance of corals, Mneba island has a 5-star PADI dive centre that offers daily diving and snorkelling trips to Mnemba Atoll and the north-east coastal reefs.
Discover scuba diving, whether as a beginner or professional diver, and dive into the most beautiful underwater world in Africa.

Cheetah's Rock

FOR THOSE WHO LOVE NATURE

A life changing experience for those who love nature, animals, and unusual travel experiences. This ethical conservation shelter is home to one of the most beautiful wild animals, the cheetah, and protects other wild animals such as pumas, zebras, and otters. All the animals have been saved from tragic circumstances.
A place where the lives of the animals matter is worth visiting, and this unique destination allows you to encounter the beauty of the animals from zero distance.
Our team will help you reach Cheetah’s Rock and enjoy this unique experience that you will remember forever.
Tours to Cheetah’s Rock are arranged in private or small groups after an appointment with the team at the shelter.

Spice Tour

ADMIRE THE BEAUTIFUL FARMS

It was Portuguese traders that first brought spices to Zanzibar in the 16th century from South American and Indian colonies. The climate of Zanzibar proved to be ideal to farm of spices. Since then, spice farms have been a success story for the economy and gastronomy of the island. Saffron, cardamon, cloves, nutmeg, green and black pepper, ginger and many other spices can be found in Zanzibar, making it one of the most exciting destinations in the world for spice lovers. In this tour, you will not only learn about the history and culture of spices in Zanzibar but you can also admire the beautiful farms, plantations, and local harvesting techniques. Expect a rich blend of aromas, flavours, colours, and experiences.

Sunset Cruise

Relax and unwind on this tour in the warm Indian Ocean characterised by breath-taking views of the colourful sky. This is the most romantic way to experience the island of Zanzibar with your loved ones or a small group of likeminded travellers. Our designated team will guide you through the various sunset cruises in order to find the one most suitable to your preference and needs. Soft drinks, snacks, and dinner can be arranged while on board.
